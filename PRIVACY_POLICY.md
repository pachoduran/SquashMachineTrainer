# Privacy Policy for Squash Machine Trainer

**Last updated: April 23, 2026**

## Overview
Squash Machine Trainer ("the App") is a Bluetooth-based controller for squash ball training machines. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

## Information Collection
The App does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

- **No personal data collected**: We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any identifying information.
- **No analytics or tracking**: The App does not use any analytics services, tracking tools, or advertising frameworks.
- **No internet connection required**: The App operates entirely offline via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to communicate with the training machine and sensor pods.
- **No accounts or registration**: The App does not require user accounts or login.

## Data Stored Locally
The App stores the following data **only on your device** (never transmitted):
- Bluetooth device identifiers (MAC addresses) of your registered training machine and sensor pods
- App preferences (language, machine type settings)
- Training session counters (launch counts)

This data is stored locally using your device's storage and is never shared with any third party.

## Bluetooth Usage
The App uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to:
- Scan for and connect to compatible training machines (JDY-32) and sensor pods (JDY-23, JDY-08)
- Send control commands to the training equipment
- Receive sensor data from pods during training sessions

Bluetooth data is exchanged only between your device and your registered training equipment.

## Third-Party Services
The App does **NOT** use any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy
The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
